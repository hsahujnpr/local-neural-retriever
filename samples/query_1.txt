To mitigate the performance bottleneck for cross-AI zone traffic, we enhanced the training job scheduler to find a 'minimum cut' when dividing the training nodes into different AI zones, reducing the cross-AI zone traffic and thus collective completion time. The scheduler does this by learning the position of GPU servers in the logical topology to recommend a rank assignment.
